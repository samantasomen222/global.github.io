THREATS TO COMPUTER SUCURITY:
A threat is a potential violation of security. When a threat is actually executed, it becomes an attack.

Computer Viruses:
They are malicious programs that cause damage to data and files on a system. They can attack on software like operating system, system areas, files, boot, etc.
1. Virus can slow down your PC, they run in background, and may cause your computer run extremely slow.
2. They can damage or delete files that are crucial to operating systems.

SPYWARES:
They are the software installed in your system to spy your activities are report this to the people who are willing to pay for it. They can be used both for illegal or legal purposes.
Typically a spyware is installed on a system from Internet when you visit particular website.
Windows operating system (OS) is more likely to fall prey to an infiltration, hackers are getting better at finding ways into Apple's OS as well.
Spyware is not just one type of program. It's an entire category of malicious software that includes adware, keyboard loggers, Trojans and mobile information stealing programs.

ADWARE:
Adware, is a term used to describe unwanted software that displays advertisements on your device.Adware’s purpose is to generate revenue for its developer and it does this by showing these ads.
When you download freeware or shareware, oftentimes adware is included. These free versions programs use adware to fund development and distribution. This type of adware doesn’t have malicious intent but can be annoying.
On-Site Adware is also referred to as browser hi-jacking. When you visit an infected website and it leads to an unauthorized installation of Adware. Then while browsing, you are bombarded with ads.

SPAMMING:
Spamming is the use of electronic messaging systems to send unwanted bulk messages indiscriminately.
Consequently, there are many organizations, as well as individuals, who have taken it upon themselves to fight spam with a variety of techniques. But because the Internet is public, there is really little that can be done to prevent spam, just as it is impossible to prevent junk mail. However, some online services have instituted policies to prevent spammers from spamming their subscribers.

->Know What You're Installing.
->Read What You Click.
->Install a Firewall.

Steps against Computer Virus

->Install Anti-Virus/Malware Software.
->Keep Your Anti-Virus Software Up to Date.
->Run Regularly Scheduled Scans with Your Anti-Virus Software.
->Keep Your Operating System Current.
->Secure Your Network.
->Think Before You Click.

Steps against adwares

Keep Updated. One of the best ways to prevent adware is to keep your system and programs updated. Adjust Your Browser Settings. Install a Firewall. Know What You're Installing. Watch What You Click.

Steps against spamming

Never give out or post your email address publicly. You should remember that everyone can easily access the Internet. Think before you click. Do not reply to spam messages. Download spam filtering tools and anti-virus software. Avoid using your personal or business email address.
